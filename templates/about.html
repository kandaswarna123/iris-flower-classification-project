About Iris Flower Classification

This website is dedicated to the classification of iris flowers using machine learning algorithms. The iris flower dataset is a popular dataset for testing machine learning algorithms, and it consists of measurements of various iris flowers' sepal length, sepal width, petal length, and petal width.

How it works

Our machine learning model analyzes the measurements of iris flowers and predicts their species based on these features. We have trained our model using a variety of machine learning techniques, including but not limited to logistic regression, decision trees, and support vector machines.

Why it matters

Understanding and accurately classifying iris flowers is not only important for botany enthusiasts but also for various fields such as agriculture, ecology, and horticulture. By automating the classification process with machine learning, we can efficiently identify and categorize iris flowers, aiding in research and conservation efforts.
